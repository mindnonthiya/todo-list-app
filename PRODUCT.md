# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who want a friendly, lightweight daily planning surface for tracking simple todos across desktop and mobile contexts. They need quick task capture, clear progress feedback, and reassuring visual organization without changing the existing CRUD workflow.

## Product Purpose

This app helps users create, read, update, delete, complete, and uncomplete todos through the existing REST API while presenting the work as a soft planner dashboard. Success means the app feels like a cute digital diary and calendar planner while preserving every current backend integration and business rule.

## Brand Personality

Warm, encouraging, and playful. The interface should feel calm and premium rather than childish: soft sage surfaces, cream paper-like backgrounds, gentle doodles, and friendly microcopy that makes productivity feel approachable.

## Anti-references

Avoid stark monochrome task managers, dense enterprise dashboards, neon gradients, harsh corners, cluttered calendar apps, and UI flourishes that obscure standard todo actions or API state.

## Design Principles

- Preserve the task flow first: every visual upgrade must keep CRUD operations obvious and reliable.
- Planner over list: frame todos inside calendar, mood, progress, and daily summary context.
- Softness with clarity: use pastel color, rounded cards, and doodles without sacrificing contrast or readable controls.
- Delight in supporting roles: decorative doodle characters and motion should reinforce state, not replace functionality.
- Mobile-first calm: the app should feel like a polished mobile planner that expands gracefully to desktop.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls, visible focus states, semantic buttons and form labels, touch-friendly hit targets, reduced-motion support, and clear empty/error/loading states.
